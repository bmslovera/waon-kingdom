New Arrival

-Series Harmony - The Gentle Spirit's Haven (July 2025)-



The Harmony Series is designed for everyone, from children to adults, who appreciate the gentle charm of light, cute colors and a sense of peaceful joy. This series focuses on creating an inviting and comforting aesthetic, perfect for everyday journaling, light note-taking, or simply adding a touch of sweetness to your day. The cane cat here embodies warmth, gentleness, and playful comfort.

The color palette for this series is composed of soft pastels: gentle mint greens, sky blues, delicate lilacs, blush pinks, and creamy yellows. Think of stationery adorned with subtle patterns of tiny hearts, stars, or floral motifs. This series includes items like cuddly plush notebooks, pens with ergonomic grips in pastel shades, decorative sticky notes, and planners with cheerful illustrations. Every piece in the Harmony Series is crafted to evoke a feeling of calm, happiness, and simple delight, bringing a little bit of Waon Kingdom's gentle magic into daily life.

Product

/Academic Series

The Scholars' Sanctuary

The Academic Series is crafted for students, scholars, and anyone on a journey of learning and discovery. It aims to inspire curiosity, facilitate organization, and make the pursuit of knowledge an even more delightful experience. The designs within this series are rooted in the classical aesthetics of Brit Fantasy's grand libraries, ancient universities, and hallowed halls of learning, with a touch of the cane cat's insightful wisdom.
/Office Series

The Professional's Polish

Designed for the modern professional, the Office Series brings a touch of Brit Fantasy sophistication and efficiency to the workplace. It's about elevating everyday office tasks with premium quality and elegant design, promoting productivity and a sense of refined order. The cane cat here represents a calm, collected, and astute presence in the bustling professional world.
/Designer Series

The Artisan's Alchemy

The Designer Series is a celebration of creativity, imagination, and artistic expression. It's tailored for artists, designers, and anyone who sees the world through a creative lens, offering tools that inspire innovation and capture flights of fancy. Here, the cane cat is the muse, the playful spirit that encourages bold strokes and imaginative leaps.
